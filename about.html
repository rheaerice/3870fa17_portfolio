Rhea Erice

Rhea Erice is a current undergraduate at California State University East Bay double majoring in Graphic Design and Multimedia.
Her passion stems from her interest in drawing and painting since she was a little girl.
Her curiosity, desire for challenge, and eye for detail inspired her to continue developing art in the digital world.

Born in the Philippines and raised in Hawaii, the Bay Area reminds her of the ocean she was always surrounded by.
She enjoys cooking, writing, and hiking during her free time.
